La valse du pixel

AUTORE: Giubra Calia - TITOLO: La valse du Pixel - ANNO: 2020

TECNICA: Video. Images and text audification.

Concept: Have you ever wonder how does an image sound like? How does a text sound like? Pixels become alive, they are not just coloured squared anymore but sounds too. The melody is produced by the images and the drum by the text. “Outside the internet there is no glory” (M. Manetas). Artworks are “informatics creatures”, electricity is the lifeblood, without connection there is no longer life.

Software: Ableton Live, Adobe Audition, Audacity, Max 8
